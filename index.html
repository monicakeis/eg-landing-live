Eastwood Global // What Parents Ask

What Parents Ask First

Six questions every family asks. Answered honestly.

01

Is an online IB actually as rigorous as a physical school?

Yes, and the results prove it. Our average IB score is 37, well above the global average of 30.88, with a 100% diploma success rate. Every class is live and teacher-led, not pre-recorded.

02

Will the IB Diploma be recognised by top universities?

The IB Diploma is widely recognised by universities and higher education institutions around the world. Our graduates are equipped for academic and career opportunities, prepared to succeed in university and beyond.

03

My child has a busy schedule: sport, travel, life. Can this work?

That’s exactly who we’re built for. Two global timetables, morning and afternoon Swiss time, give families the flexibility to plan around training, competitions, and time zones without sacrificing academic rigour.

04

Can my child join mid-year, or start from Grade 10?

We offer rolling admissions with entry in Semester 1 and Semester 2. Students in Grade 10 can enrol ahead of the DP to ensure a seamless transition into the full IB Diploma Programme.

05

What does a real school community look like online?

Live, interactive classes led by expert IB educators. Through dynamic discussions, immediate feedback, and interactive group work, students develop the critical thinking and collaboration skills needed to thrive in the IB and beyond.

06

What support is there for university applications?

University preparation is built into the programme from day one. Subject selection, the Extended Essay (EE), and CAS activities are all guided with long-term university fit in mind, not left to the final year.

Eastwood Global // IB Diploma Curriculum

Six subject groups.
One globally recognised diploma.

Every student takes six subjects, three at Higher Level, three at Standard Level, plus the three core components: Theory of Knowledge (TOK), Extended Essay (EE), and Creativity, Activity, Service (CAS).

Group 1 — Studies in Language & Literature HL/SL
Arabic A · English A · German A · Italian A · Spanish A · Self-taught Language (SL)
Group 2 — Language Acquisition HL/SL
Arabic B · Arabic ab initio · English B · French B · French ab initio · German B · Italian B · Spanish B · Spanish ab initio
Group 3 — Individuals & Societies HL/SL
History · Economics · Business Management · Psychology · Global Politics
Group 4 — Sciences HL/SL

Group 5 — Mathematics HL/SL
